OUR HERITAGE

A house built on restraint

Maison Verlaine opened as a single atelier with four tailors and one rule: nothing leaves the workshop until it's right. That rule never changed, even as the house grew.

We still work in small seasonal runs, still fit every sample on a live model before it's approved, and still finish every hem, cuff, and buttonhole by hand.

— Paris, est. 1998
THE ATELIER

What we won't compromise on

Craftsmanship

Every garment passes through the hands of a single lead tailor from cut to final press.

Material

Fabrics are sourced from mills we've worked with for over a decade, chosen for hand-feel first.

Longevity

Every piece is built to be repaired, not replaced — we alter and restore Maison Verlaine pieces for life.
