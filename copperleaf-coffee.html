OUR STORY

A small room, taken seriously

Copperleaf started as a single roaster in the back of a bookshop. We kept the batches small on purpose — it's the only way to taste every adjustment and catch every bag before it leaves the room.

Today it's a full café, but the habit never left: beans roasted on site, pastries baked before sunrise, and a bar staffed by people who taste everything before they pour it for you.
